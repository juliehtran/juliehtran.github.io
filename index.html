Hello! I'm Julie.

I am a full stack software engineer who codes for a better human experience. Before I chose this path, I worked as a graphic designer for small businesses in Boston. Creating graphics, marketing and web assets for close-knit communities gave me the inspiration to code. I found myself wishing desperately to bridge the gap between design and software engineering to enhance the online presence of my clients. I approach problems with a strong sense of curiosity and excitement. What motivates me is collaborating and working with thinkers, creators, and self-starters to produce something innovative and meaningful. When I'm not coding, I may have my head buried in painting a new art piece, playing with my shih tzu, Mimi, or spending time with my four other siblings. I have a particular interest in video games, horror films, and karaoke.
